The Challenge

For CEAT Tyres' centenary, the "Beyond That Horizon" campaign retraced the brand's roots from India back to Italy — a cinematic road trip spanning multiple countries, cultures, and teams. I was entrusted with the entire French production leg.

My task: anchor a moving international crew in a country they didn't know, bridge the gap between an Indian creative vision and a French operational reality, and deliver cinematic results — daily.

Logistics Location Scouting Crew Management Schedule Management Cross-Cultural Liaison

The Execution

Working alongside creators Mithila Palkar and Varun Aditya, I managed real-time problem solving across the South of France and French Alps — securing locations, managing crew movements, and ensuring the CEAT SportDrive series was showcased against world-class backdrops.

It was my job to handle every behind-the-scenes complexity so that the camera could focus entirely on delivering cinematic precision. The result: a multi-part YouTube series that brought CEAT's centenary story to millions of viewers globally.
